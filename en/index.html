About

Just like home, a stone's throw from the sea

La Finestra Blu is a new, completely renovated B&B in Civitanova Marche (MC), in the Fontespina district. Located right in front of the sea and not far from the town center and shopping.

Welcoming, inclusive, and family-run, the structure is ideal for pleasant and relaxing stays with every comfort you could hope for. It offers three rooms, including one with accessibility features: CAREZZA, SORRISO, and ABBRACCIO.
